The Entertainer - ESFP-T

Introduction

Entertainer types are spontanious, energetic and enthusiastic people. They can get caught up in the moment, but they want everyone else to feel that way too. They have the most time generous personsality and can be extremely encouraging towards others. Entertainers can become so focused on the immediate pleasures that they can often neglect the tasks that make those luxuries possible. Entertainers can also recognise value and quality when they see it, this however, can lead to them living beyond the capabilities, such as spending too much.

Strengths and Weaknesses

Entertainers are bold, and they aren't known for holding back. They want to experience everything.
